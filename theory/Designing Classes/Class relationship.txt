Inheritance
* Is-a relationship
* Car is a Vehicle

Aggregation
* Has-a relationship
* Car has a tire
* Related to "Composition"
* aggregation can “live” independently
    - A Tire can exist with even without a Car

Composition
* composition cannot “live” independently
    - A Room cannot exist without a House

Dependency
* Uses relationship
* weaker than aggregation
* Example: many of our applications depend on the Scanner class to read input
